Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 22, 2026

At Sauce, your peace of mind is our priority. This policy outlines how we handle data.

1. Local-First Data

Sauce is designed to be "dead simple" and private. All your data, including your name, selected "Spirit," and "Path," is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage and SQLite. We do not have a backend server; your data never leaves your phone.

2. Notifications

We use local notifications to deliver your daily proverb. These do not track your location or usage habits.

3. Third-Party Services

We use RevenueCat for future monetization placeholders, which may collect anonymous device identifiers to manage subscriptions.
